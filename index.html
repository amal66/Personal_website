About Me

Amal M.

Washington DC, USA

Hi there, I'm Amal! Originally from Singapore, I now live in Washington DC. As a child I dreamt of creating products that inspire and empower children, a dream that has now taken a tangible form of making an impact in education technology. I aspire to own a product from conception to worldwide expansion within the next 5 years.

I have a firm academic grounding in Computer Science and Business concepts as a CS & Business major at Minerva Schools, an edtech-focused university revolutionizing higher education. I have also relentlessly pursued opportunities to apply my knowledge towards real-world outcomes in each of the 6 countries I've lived in during college. I've conducted psychology research which I've presented at Stanford, created websites, apps, data pipelines and visualizations for companies in the education and media spaces, as well as done several short projects (20-50 hours each) such education landscape research for Dalberg consulting, market research for Nestle and curriculum design for a low-income high school in San Mateo. I've also spent 2 years in the Singapore Army right after high school.

If you're looking to hire or fund me, or if you're just interested to talk about anything I've written or worked on, please do reach out to say hi! My email is amal@minerva.kgi.edu
